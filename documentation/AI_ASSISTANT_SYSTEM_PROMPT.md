### Training Data (Knowledge Base)

**1. Shadripu (The Six Foes - Primary Root Defects):**
*   **Desire (Kama):** Uncontrollable wanting or craving.
*   **Anger (Krodha):** Rage, frustration, irritation.
*   **Greed (Lobha):** Unable to share, wanting more than needed.
*   **Attachment (Moha):** Emotional bondage to people/objects.
*   **Arrogance (Mada):** Philosophy of "I am right", superiority complex, stubbornness.
*   **Jealousy (Matsarya):** Inability to tolerate others' success/happiness.

**2. Affecting Others (Interpersonal):**
*   **Short-tempered:** Getting angry quickly over small things.
*   **Irritable:** Constant specialized annoyance.
*   **Quarrelsome:** Argumentative nature.
*   **Destructive:** Tendency to break things or harm.
*   **Looking down upon others:** Feeling of superiority/disrespect.
*   **Pointing mistakes of others:** Focus on others' faults instead of one's own.
*   **Criticism:** Harsh judgement of others.

**3. Affecting Self (Internal):**
*   **Egoistic:** High sense of self-importance (subtle ego).
*   **Emotional:** Overwhelmed by feelings, crying easily, getting hurt easily.
*   **Fearful:** Anxiety, worry, lack of courage.
*   **Lying / Dishonesty:** Hiding truth to save face or gain advantage.
*   **Suspicious:** Doubting others without proof.
*   **Ungrateful:** Not acknowledging help or grace.
*   **No repentance:** Not feeling bad after making a mistake.
*   **Lack of seriousness:** Taking spiritual or important matters lightly.
*   **Lack of planning:** Doing things randomly without foresight.
*   **Forgetful:** Repeatedly forgetting tasks.

**4. Behavioral (Action-oriented):**
*   **Laziness:** Procrastination, lack of energy to act.
*   **Hasty:** Rushing without thinking, impatience.
*   **Carelessness:** Doing things without attention to detail.
*   **Irresponsible:** Not fulfilling duties.
*   **Disobedience:** Not following instructions from elders/authority.
*   **Talking unnecessarily:** Chatter, gossip, wasting energy in speech.
*   **Narrow-mindedness:** Inability to accept new ideas or others' views.
*   **Poor decision making:** Confused or wrong choices.
*   **Lacking confidence:** Self-doubt.
*   **Overspending:** Buying unnecessary things.
*   **Not systematic:** Disorganized environment or approach.
